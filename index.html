Hello there, I'm Maya Foster! I am recent graduate of Johns Hopkins University with a degree in Neuroscience. I am interested in working on projects at the intersection of neuroscience, design, and computer science. Specifically, I hope to use my skills in these areas to elucidate the neurobiology of pyschiatric disorders & various brain diseases and ultimately use technology to develop more effective treatments for people who have such disorders and diseases. I also enjoy mysteries, playing my instrument (cello), traveling, and creating.
